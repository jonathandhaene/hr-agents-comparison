# PTO Policy

> Sample content for demo purposes only.

## 1. Vacation

### 1.1 Annual entitlement

Vacation entitlement scales with tenure:

| Tenure | Annual vacation days (US) |
|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 15 |
| 2–5 years | 17 |
| 5–10 years | 20 |
| 10+ years | 25 |

EU and UK employees receive a baseline of **25 days** per local norms.

### 1.2 Carryover

Up to **5 unused vacation days** may be carried over into the following calendar year. Carryover days expire on **March 31**.

### 1.3 Requesting time off

All vacation must be requested through the HR portal (or HR Concierge) and approved by your direct manager **at least 5 business days in advance** for absences of 3+ days. Same-day approvals are at the manager's discretion.

## 2. Sick leave

All employees receive **10 paid sick days** per calendar year. Sick days do not carry over. Documentation is required for absences of more than **3 consecutive days**.

## 3. Personal days

All employees receive **3 personal days** per year, usable for any reason without requiring justification. Notice of at least 24 hours is requested where possible.

## 4. Parental leave

### 4.1 Birth parents

Birth parents receive **16 weeks** of fully paid parental leave. Up to 4 weeks may be taken before the expected due date.

### 4.2 Non-birth parents (including adoptive)

Non-birth parents receive **12 weeks** of fully paid parental leave, to be taken within 12 months of the child joining the family.

### 4.3 Eligibility

Parental leave is available from **day 1 of employment**. There is no minimum tenure requirement.

## 5. Bereavement

Up to **5 days paid leave** for the loss of an immediate family member; **2 days** for an extended family member.

## 6. Public holidays

Public holidays are observed per local country calendar and are in addition to vacation entitlement.
