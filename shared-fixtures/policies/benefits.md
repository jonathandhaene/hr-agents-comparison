# Benefits Overview

> Sample content for demo purposes only.

## 1. Health insurance (US)

Zava offers three medical plan options through our partner carrier:

- **Plan A — PPO** (broad network, higher premiums, lower deductible)
- **Plan B — HDHP + HSA** (lower premiums, higher deductible, employer HSA contribution of $1,000 individual / $2,000 family)
- **Plan C — HMO** (lowest premiums, in-network only, primary care required)

Zava pays **80%** of the monthly premium for the employee and **70%** for dependents.

## 2. Dental & vision

Standard dental (preventive, basic, major) and vision (annual exam, lenses, frames allowance) are available. Zava pays **75%** of the dental premium and **100%** of the vision premium.

## 3. Mental health & wellness

- **Headspace** subscription provided to all employees and household members.
- **Modern Health** EAP: 8 free coaching sessions and 8 free therapy sessions per year.
- Annual **wellness stipend** of $500 reimbursable for fitness, nutrition, ergonomic equipment.

## 4. Family-forming benefits

- Up to **$25,000 lifetime** for fertility, adoption, and surrogacy expenses.
- Backup childcare credit: **10 days per year** through our partner.

## 5. Learning & development

- Annual **L&D budget** of $2,000 per employee for courses, books, conferences.
- Free unlimited access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera Plus, O'Reilly.

## 6. Retirement (US)

See the [Employee Handbook](./employee-handbook.md) section 3.3.

## 7. Equity

Most employees receive an annual equity refresh as part of the H1 compensation cycle. Vesting is **4-year, 1-year cliff** for new grants and quarterly thereafter.
